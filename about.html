My Story

I am Stephanie Montoya, an immigration attorney licensed in Florida, Texas, and New York, with experience dedicated exclusively to immigration law. I was born in Miami to immigrant parents, and that experience shaped my deep commitment to helping others navigate the U.S. immigration system with dignity and confidence.

Throughout my career, I have represented hundreds of clients in cases ranging from family-based petitions and employment visas to complex deportation defense and asylum proceedings. I speak English and Spanish, — ensuring you are always heard and understood in your language.

My Philosophy

I believe that every person deserves access to quality legal representation, regardless of their immigration status or background. Behind every case is a human story — and I take that responsibility seriously. I work personally with each client, maintaining open communication and setting realistic expectations from day one.

My goal is not just to win your case — it is to give you the peace of mind and stability you and your family deserve.
