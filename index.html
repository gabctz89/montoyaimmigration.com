About Me

An attorney who truly understands your story

Stephanie Montoya is the founder of her law practice and a dedicated advocate in immigration and criminal defense law. With extensive experience in visas, waivers, and deportation defense, she is known for her strategic approach and unwavering commitment to her clients.

Her passion for immigration law is deeply personal. From a young age, she felt called to serve immigrant communities—a mission that led her to live in Mexico, where she strengthened her cultural insight and built meaningful connections within the Latino community.

Stephanie's academic background includes a Juris Doctor, a Master's degree in Criminal Justice, and a Bachelor's degree in Psychology with a specialization in Spanish. Fully bilingual in English and Spanish, she provides accessible, compassionate, and results-driven representation.

When she is not advocating for her clients, Stephanie enjoys traveling and experiencing new cultures—values that continue to shape her global perspective and her work.
