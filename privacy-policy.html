Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Stephanie Montoya Law LLC ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide when you visit our website or contact us through our forms.

2. Information We Collect

When you submit the contact form on our website, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Description of your legal situation

3. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to:

  • Respond to your consultation request
  • Evaluate your legal situation
  • Contact you regarding your case

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Attorney-Client Confidentiality

Any information shared with our office is treated with strict confidentiality in accordance with applicable attorney-client privilege rules. Submitting a contact form does not establish an attorney-client relationship.

5. Data Security

We use Formspree to process contact form submissions. Your data is transmitted securely and stored in accordance with their privacy practices. We implement reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

You have the right to:

To exercise these rights, please contact us at stephanie@montoyaimmigration.com.
